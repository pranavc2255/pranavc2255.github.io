About

I am a PhD researcher in the Department of Civil, Architectural, and Environmental Engineering at Drexel University. My work focuses on bridging robotics, AI, and structural engineering to enable autonomous, data-driven inspection of built infrastructure. I build complete systems — from custom end-effectors and sensing pipelines to learning-based decision and control.

Research Areas

Robotic Ultrasonic Testing

Autonomous UPV inspection using UR manipulators, force-aware control, and custom transducer end-effectors.

Vision-Language-Action Models

Learning perception-to-action policies for complex, contact-rich robotic tasks.

Autonomous Mapping & Perception

ROS-based RGB-D perception, mapping, and decision-making systems for unstructured environments.
